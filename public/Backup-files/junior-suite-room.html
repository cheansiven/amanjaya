JUNIOR SUITES

Every Junior Suites is overlooking the river. With 45 sqm of living space, they are expansive and relaying a deep feeling of comfort with their king size bed and lounge area.

To make your stay a real Cambodian experience, we have handpicked details, such as: floor fitted from local rosewood and black-out curtains are made from famous Cambodian raw silk. Each suite is decorated with modern reproduction of artefacts from the neighbouring National Museum.
